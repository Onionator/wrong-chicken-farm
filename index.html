    Transactions

  • Buy 10 feed for $7
  • Chickens sell for $10

    Win Condition

  • Keep the chickens alive until you get to $100!

    How to play

  • Click a chicken to select it. If a chicken is selected it will be yellow.
  • When a chicken gets to a feed count of 9 it spawns another chicken.
  • If a chicken's death timer reaches 0 it dies.
  • Feed a chicken to add to its feed counter and reset its death timer.

    Lose Conditions

  • If you have no chickens you lose.
  • If you don't have any living chickens you lose.
